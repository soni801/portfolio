About me

Hi! My name is Samuel, and I go by the username Soni online. I studied IT and media production at Amalie Skram VGS in Bergen, Norway during 2020-2022. I now work full-time as an IT technician at Høgskulen på Vestlandet.

I got introduced to programming in 2016 and did block programming for some time before learning web development. After some time I also started writing Java. I now have knowledge in several programming, scripting and markup languages.

My interests heavily revolve around computers and include programming, digital media production and gaming. I also have interests and hobbies outside computers, like speedcubing.

I've worked with web and front end development for several years, with additional backend knowledge. I have also done game design and development, in addition to a bit of graphic design.

I can do frontend and backend web development, game development, mobile and desktop app design and development, logo design, general branding, color grading and video editing. If you want to discuss a business opportunity, feel free to contact me.
